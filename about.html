Subhrendu Chattopadhyay

Research Scholar
Department of Computer Scienece and Engineering
Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati

Subhrendu Chattopadhyay received his M.Tech from Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati in India in 2014 under supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi. Currently he is enrolled as a PhD student in the Dept. of CSE, IIT Guwahati, India. His thesis adviser is Prof. Sukumar Nandi. His research interests include Software Defined Networking, Distributed Algorithms, Performance Modelling of Network and Communication System and Wireless Mesh Network.
