Subhrendu Chattopadhyay

Temporary Project Staff
Department of Computer Science and Engineering
Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur

Subhrendu Chattopadhyay is a temporary project staff in Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT-Kgp). Prior to this assignment, he was a research scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG). Recently he has submitted his thesis titled "SDN for Large Scale IoT Networks" which is under examination. During his Ph.D. he has been working under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi and the mentorship of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty. He received his M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from the IITG in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi. Currently he is working as a Temporary Project Staff in Dept. of CSE, IIT Kharagpur under the mentorship of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty. Subhrendu's research interests include Software Defined Networking, Distributed Algorithms, Performance Modelling of Network and Communication System and Wireless Mesh Network. Currently he is exploring (a). resource allocation for RAN services in Fog networking, (b). Vulnerability analysis from System Logs .
