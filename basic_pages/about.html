Subhrendu Chattopadhyay, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor
Department of Computer Science and Engineering
SRM-University, AP

Subhrendu is an Assistant Professor in SRM-University, AP in the department of CSE. Prior to this assignment, he has worked as a Temporary project staff at IIT Kharagpur under the mentorship of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty. He completed his Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG) and his thesis is titled "SDN for Large Scale IoT Networks". During his Ph.D. he has been working under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi and the mentorship of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty. Subhrendu received his M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from the IITG in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi. Subhrendu's research interests include Software Defined Networking, Distributed Algorithms, Performance Modelling of Network and Communication System and Wireless Mesh Network. Currently he is exploring (a). Resource allocation and scheduling of RAN services over Fog networking, (b). Vulnerability analysis from System Logs, and (c). Performance enhancement of DASH by utilizing VNF.
