Subhrendu Chattopadhyay

Temporary Project Staff
Department of Computer Science and Engineering
Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur

Subhrendu Chattopadhyay was associated with the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG) as a research scholar. Recently he has submitted his thesis titled "SDN for Large Scale IoT Networks" which is under examination. During his Ph.D. he has been working under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi and the mentorship of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty. He received his M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from the IITG in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi. Currently he is working as a Temporary Project Staff in Dept. of CSE, IIT Kharagpur under the mentorship of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty. Subhrendu's research interests include Software Defined Networking, Distributed Algorithms, Performance Modelling of Network and Communication System and Wireless Mesh Network.
